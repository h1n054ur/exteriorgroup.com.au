Accessibility

Working to improve accessibility to our website and strive to create an accessible and barrier-free environment.

Our Commitment

Men In Kilts is committed to providing individuals with disabilities access to information, goods, services and privileges offered on this website, https://www.meninkilts.com/, and is in an ongoing process of improving its accessibility. We are making every effort to provide a website in which functionality and content is accessible to all individuals, and we are actively updating and monitoring our website to make it as accessible as possible.
